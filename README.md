# Foreshortening Aid

A cross-platform tool designed to help artists understand and draw foreshortened human forms more accurately.

Available on desktop, iOS, and Android.

---

## What it does

Foreshortening can make even simple poses feel impossible.

This tool helps you visualize how forms compress and extend in space so you can draw them with more confidence.

* supports difficult viewing angles
* improves spatial awareness
* helps translate 3D form into 2D drawing

---

## Platforms

* Desktop
* iOS
* Android

---

## Why it exists

Foreshortening is one of the most frustrating parts of figure drawing.

This was built to make it easier to see what’s actually happening, not just guess.

## Philosophy

Drawing is not about perfection.

It’s about understanding what you’re seeing.

This tool is here to help you see it.
